
## 2024-11-20 - Custom React Toggle Button Groups and Icon-Only Buttons Accessibility
**Learning:** In AcordesAI, custom toggle button groups (like the instrument selector) lacked `role="group"`, `aria-label`, and `aria-pressed` attributes, making their state unclear to screen readers. Furthermore, icon-only buttons (like Favorite, Comments, and Font Size controls) lacked explicit `type="button"`, localized `aria-label` attributes, tooltips (`title`), and focus indicators, impairing keyboard navigability and accessibility.
**Action:** When implementing custom toggle button groups, always wrap the buttons in a container with `role="group"` and an `aria-label`, and use the `aria-pressed` attribute on individual toggle buttons to denote active state. For icon-only buttons, ensure they have explicit `type="button"`, localized `aria-label`, `title`, and `focus-visible` styling (`focus-visible:ring-2 focus-visible:ring-brand focus-visible:outline-none`) for proper accessibility and form behavior.
