## 2024-03-21 - Accessible Icon-Only Buttons and Toggle Groups
**Learning:** Icon-only buttons require explicit `aria-label` and `title` attributes for screen readers and tooltips, along with `type="button"` and focus styles. Furthermore, for instrument selectors acting as a toggle group, wrapping them in a container with `role="group"` and an `aria-label`, and adding `aria-pressed` states and focus styles to the buttons, significantly improves screen reader navigation. Decorative or non-interactive icons (e.g., Lucide React icons used for visual context) should use `aria-hidden="true"` to prevent screen reader confusion.
**Action:** Always add `aria-label`, `title`, `type="button"`, and focus styles (`focus-visible:ring-2 focus-visible:ring-brand focus-visible:outline-none`) to icon-only buttons. Use `role="group"`, `aria-pressed`, and `aria-hidden="true"` where applicable.
