## 2024-03-20 - Accessible Icon Controls & Instrument Selector
**Learning:** Icon-only buttons (like favorites, comments, zoom, autoscroll) and segmented controls (like the instrument selector) in the `SongViewer` were completely lacking screen-reader accessible names, states, and keyboard focus indicators, creating a significant barrier for users navigating the application without a mouse. Specifically, the instrument selector buttons lacked `aria-pressed` states and group semantics.
**Action:** When implementing custom interactive controls, always include `aria-label` or `title` on icon-only buttons, add `aria-hidden="true"` to purely decorative SVG icons to prevent redundant announcements, provide explicit `type="button"` attributes to prevent unwanted form submissions, and use `focus-visible` utility classes to ensure robust keyboard navigation. Segmented controls should use `role="group"` with an `aria-label` on the container and `aria-pressed` on the toggle buttons.
