## 2026-03-22 - Accessible Interactive Icons and Instrument Toggles
**Learning:** Found an accessibility issue pattern in AcordesAI specifically regarding icon-only buttons (like favorites and comments) and instrument selectors (Guitar, Ukulele, Piano). Icon-only buttons lacked ARIA labels and focus rings. Instrument selectors lacked group roles and accessible active states (aria-pressed). Decorative icons within buttons were also being read by screen readers.
**Action:** Always add `aria-label`, `title`, `type="button"`, and `focus-visible` utility classes to icon-only buttons. Add `aria-hidden="true"` to decorative inner icons. For instrument/category toggle buttons, wrap them in a container with `role="group"` and `aria-label`, and use `aria-pressed` to denote active states, to ensure smooth keyboard navigation and screen reader support.
