## 2024-05-24 - Accessible Icon Buttons in Dark Theme
**Learning:** In a heavily customized dark theme (like Tailwind's `bg-dark-*`), default browser focus rings are often invisible or have poor contrast against the background. Furthermore, `lucide-react` icons within icon-only buttons can be read confusingly by screen readers if they lack `aria-hidden="true"`, while the buttons themselves need descriptive `aria-label` and `title` attributes.
**Action:** Always apply `focus-visible:ring-2 focus-visible:ring-brand focus-visible:outline-none` (using the theme's high-contrast brand color) to all interactive elements to ensure keyboard accessibility. Additionally, ensure all icon-only buttons have localized `aria-label` and `title` attributes, and apply `aria-hidden="true"` to the decorative icon SVG to prevent redundant or confusing screen reader announcements.
