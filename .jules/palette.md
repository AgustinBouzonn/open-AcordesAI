## 2024-05-24 - Add ARIA Labels and Titles to Icon-Only Buttons in SongViewer
**Learning:** Icon-only buttons must include `aria-label` for screen readers, `title` for tooltips, explicit `type='button'` attributes, and `focus-visible:ring-2 focus-visible:ring-brand focus-visible:outline-none` classes to ensure accessibility, keyboard navigability, and proper form behavior.
**Action:** Always verify that icon-only buttons have these attributes. Ensure consistent focus states for keyboard users.
